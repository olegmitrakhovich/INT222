Audio Implementation in HTML5
This is an Audio Implementation in HTML5, as you can see it's a very simple play bar that plays MP3 or an OGG type files. There are numerous types of sound files your web page can use, but MP3 and OGG are most popular. If the client can't play a certain version of the audio file, HTML5 will use any alternative version of the sound file that the developer identified in his or her code.
